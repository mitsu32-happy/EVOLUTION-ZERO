# Dino Roster Spec

## MVP-A01: Spinosaurus

- Added `spinosaurus` as the fourth playable dinosaur for post-release content.
- Role: mid-range control using water blades, splash, and vortex pressure.
- Unlock: research item `spinosaurus_unlock` in the Unknown Domain category.
- Save key: `unlockedDinos.spinosaurus.unlocked`.
- Default roster remains Velociraptor / Triceratops / Tyrannosaurus; Spinosaurus is locked until research unlock or `debugUnlockDino=spinosaurus`.

## Future Additions

- The fifth and sixth dinosaurs are not implemented in MVP-A01.
- `spinosaurus_zero` is defined as a future `ruins ZERO CLEAR` route, but ruins ZERO remains locked and does not grant this reward in the current public flow.
