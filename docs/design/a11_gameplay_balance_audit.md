# MVP-A11 Gameplay Balance Audit

Date: 2026-06-04

## Scope

MVP-A11 focuses only on gameplay feel:

- Enemy density pressure
- Enemy and boss damage pressure
- Difficulty differentiation
- Adaptation skill growth
- Base dinosaur identity
- Normal evolution benefit
- ZERO evolution reward feel

No UI, PWA, asset, result, boss notice, or new dinosaur work is included.

## Current Feel Summary

### NORMAL

- Early game already has enough enemy presence for first-time readability.
- Contact damage remains forgiving, which supports爽快感.
- Main risk is that late NORMAL can feel too flat once adaptation skills begin cycling.

Classification:

- 爽快感: acceptable
- 成長感: needs stronger skill level scaling
- 難易度: should remain approachable

### HARD

- Enemy count and stage enemy mix create pressure, but contact damage did not clearly separate HARD from NORMAL.
- Mid and late phases needed slightly sharper damage and density.

Classification:

- 難易度不足
- 終盤圧不足

### EXPERT

- Enemy variants and spawn rate are present, but late pressure could still be broken through too easily.
- Damage needed a larger step up so mistakes feel costly without becoming instant-death.

Classification:

- 難易度不足
- 終盤圧不足

### ENDLESS

- Scaling exists, but post-600s pressure was too gentle for a survival mode.
- Long-run density and damage needed a stronger slope.

Classification:

- 終盤圧不足
- 達成感不足

### ZERO

- Multi-boss structure and ZERO notices are in place.
- Enemy and boss pressure needed a stronger late ramp.
- ZERO evolution is already visually special, but combat payoff needed more range, damage, target count, and boss relevance.

Classification:

- ZERO特別感不足
- 終盤圧不足
- 達成感不足

## Tuning Targets

- Keep early enemy counts close to current play.
- Shift added density toward mid, late, ENDLESS, and ZERO.
- Make HARD/EXPERT damage more distinct.
- Make adaptation skill levels visibly improve output, area, target count, or cooldown.
- Make normal evolution feel like a clear power spike.
- Make ZERO evolution feel distinctly above normal evolution while ZERO difficulty remains threatening.
